Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Overview

Quy Nhon Life is a vessel tracking and local guide website for Quy Nhon, Vietnam. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate the website:

  • No personal accounts: We do not require registration or collect personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Language preference: Your selected language is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) so the site remembers your choice. This data never leaves your device.
  • Location data (Vessel Identifier feature only): If you use the "Identify a Vessel" feature, your device's GPS location and compass heading are used temporarily to match nearby vessels. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or stored.

Vessel Data

The vessel information displayed on this website is received via AIS (Automatic Identification System) radio signals, which are publicly broadcast by vessels as required by international maritime regulations. This data includes vessel positions, names, types, and other navigational information that vessels voluntarily transmit on public radio frequencies.

Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. The only browser storage used is localStorage for your language preference.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • MapTiler: Provides map tiles. MapTiler may collect anonymized usage data according to their own privacy policy.
  • Open-Meteo: Provides weather data. No personal data is shared with Open-Meteo.
  • Cloudflare: Provides DNS and security services. Cloudflare may process standard web request data (IP address, browser type) according to their privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

This website does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal data from any users, there is no specific risk to children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the website evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address listed on our website.
